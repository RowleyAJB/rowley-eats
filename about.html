What this is

I end up recommending the same handful of places to friends often enough that it made sense to just put them all in one spot. This is that spot: a list of places I rate, what I'd order if you went, and anything worth knowing before you turn up.

Mostly Wirral and Liverpool for now, since that's where I spend most of my time. It'll grow as I try new places elsewhere.

Each card shows the price band in the top corner:

£ — cheap, casual
££ — mid-range
£££ — treat yourself

Tap the Instagram icon on any card to see their latest food and opening times.

If you've got a recommendation you think should be on here, let me know and I'll go and try it.
