Put your own photos here, e.g.

  images/free-state-kitchen.jpg
  images/rudys.jpg
  images/hardware.jpg
  images/dough-and-doppio.jpg
  images/dope-crusts.jpg

Then in data.js, set the "image" field for that spot to "images/free-state-kitchen.jpg" (etc).
If "image" is left blank, a coloured placeholder is shown instead.

Tip: square or 4:3 photos work best. Anything you've taken on your phone of the food is perfect.
